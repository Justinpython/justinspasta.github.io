About Me


I grew up in my parents' restaurants, always surrounded by authentic Italian cooking and food. I was lucky enough to be in an environment where food was being prepared, and have been doing it myself ever since I was old enough to hold a rolling pin.


I went on to work in my parent's restaurant, although tended to eat more than I worked. Still, it was inspiring to be around people passionate about good food, and although the restaurants have since closed and we have all moved on, that passion runs deep.

PASTA WORKSHOP

fun with eggs and flour


I offer pasta workshops out of my Edinburgh based flat, giving people a chance to learn the basics, or develop their skills further. Based in Edinburgh's historic and vibrant Leith neighbourhood.

DETAILS

Time: 11am

Duration: 2 hours(approx.)



The workshop involves looking at some of the science behind good pasta dough putting it into practice, before creating a number of well known pasta shapes such as tagliatelle and fettucine. Using seasonal ingredients, the technique of preparing stuffed pasta such as ravioli, tortellini and cardinali is also demonstrated.
